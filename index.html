About Galaxy Africa

After a successful Galaxy Africa 2018 in South Africa, we are pleased to announce Galaxy Africa 2019 workshop in collaboration with the ASBCB/ISCB Africa conference in Kumasi, Ghana.

Building on the previous event, we will offer Galaxy training for researchers, and training for systems administrators who need to maintain a local installation.
